Lease To Me app

Lease to Me is a web portal that provides business owners retail solutions. This webapp is a scaled down proof-of-concept version of the Lease-to-Me website built by the author to showcase the recommendation service at Lease-to-Me.

Finding the right location and conditions for your new business is a daunting task. Several factors like foot traffic and local real estate prices need to be considered while choosing your business location. Market research on these factors are often not easily available and this data can often change with time. In addition, often the best business deals and other minor real estate data are often only known to low level grassroots' brokers. Lease-to-Me is a web portal and a online brokerage service that connects landlords of commercial real-estate to prospective tenants.

Lets say, you want to open a business in New York. You would like to look for the best locations for your business. In doing so, you would have to make a complicated decision involving multiple decisions. Just some of these factors include the type of business, foot traffic associated, local demographics, competing businesses, tax rates etc. Its hard for a individual business owner to pool this data. This app(Lease-to-Me) allows you to look up things like real estate prices, demographics of the neighborhood and other competing in your area that would make your business profitable. Through Lease-to-Me, you can fill in your requirements, browse the available property lisitngs as well as make contact with local brokers of the properties. This app provides an addition service of recommendations, which would help in writing leasing proposals in addition to making estimates on the viability of your future business.

Visualization: This app allows you to visualize three of the factors that determine the best location for your business:
  1. Demographics information for each zipcode: Factors such as median income and population density displayed per zipcode. Demographics and community information provides information into the ability of the local community to support your business. It potentially affects the scale of your establishment. Please click here to see the demographics visualization for your business :
  2. Real-estate information: Real estate information such as the value of the land and the last sale price are aggregated via its mean value and tiled into hexagons on the geographical regions.
  3. Places of interest : Depending on the type of business you looking for, the symbiotic businesses and competing business are displayed in the vicinity of your location.



Data Engineering:
  1. Property data : This is the property listings available through Lease-to-Me.

  2. Third party data : Tax assessment or real-estate data, places_of_interest data and demographics information was obtained from Cherre API. This data was pulled using graph QL.
  3. Tenant Requirement data : This data was also available through Lease-to-Me.

Recommendation service: A scaled down version of the app at Lease-to-Me is provided here to showcase the recommendation serivce. This allows users to fill in a survey some basic questions on the potential business/store and delivers a list of recommendations via a Suitability Score. This builds a feature space of the all the relevant attributes (categorical for property features and numeric for places of interest distances) pertaining to a specific property listing and sends out the best listing using the Gower's distance to mediate between the various distance for the attributes.
