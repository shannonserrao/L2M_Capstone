Lease To Me app

Lease to Me is a web portal that provides business owners retail solutions. Finding the right location and conditions for your new business is a daunting task. Finding retail spaces is much more challenging that renting a home.

Lets say, you want to open a business in New York. This app allows you to look up things like retail prices, demographics of the neighborhood and other businesses in your area that would make your business profitable.
